Lunch Program and Transportation Info

Lunch Program:

Click here to view next month’s lunch menu order form! Please make sure to turn in this form by its deadline to ensure timely and accurate lunch orders for your student(s).

Avalon bag lunches are provided by our contracted caterer, Done Right Foods.

Do you have questions about lunch ordering? Please contact our front office by calling 651.649.5495 ext. 200 or by e-mailing Becca at becca@avalonschool.org

Transportation Info:

After a student and guardian completes and submits Avalon’s Transportation Enrollment Form to the front office, either a Metro Transit unlimited ride or 10-ride bus card will be provided to students. 10-ride passes will be issued weekly on Tuesdays, unless otherwise specified by the front office.

Have you lost or damaged your bus pass? Replacement pass costs are as follows: $30 for a new unlimited ride pass $13.50 for a new 10-ride bus pass

Please report lost or stolen bus passes immediately so that they can be suspended or deactivated. You may purchase a new card at any time from the front office manager.

Questions about our transportation rules or Metro Transit bus passes in general? Please contact Becca in the front office at 651.649.5495 ext. 200 or e-mail her at becca@avalonschool.org
